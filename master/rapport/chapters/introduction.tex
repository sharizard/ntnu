\section{Thesis Structure}
The thesis is structured into chapters with sections and subsections. The outline of the thesis is as follows:
\begin{itemize}
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 1}}: Introction contains a brief and general introduction to the study and the motivaton behind it.
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 2}}: State-of-the-Art looks at important aspects of the research question.
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 3}}: Research Method desribes how the literature review was carried out throughout the research, as well as a description of the case study to be performed.
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 4}}: Results presents the results from the case study, and takes a closer look at the findings from the case study.
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 5}}: Discussion contains a summarized look at the findings from the case study, and connects it with the ltierature review and to the research questions. An evaluation of the research is also given in this chapter.
	\item{\textbf{Chapter 6}}: Concluision concludes the research by providing a summary of the most important points of the results and discussion chapter. Additionally, in oulines possible routes to take in the research field.
\end{itemize}
